DIFFERENCES BETWEEN PADDING, MARGINS AND BORDERS

  • Padding is the space that lies between the element content and element border, it is used to generate space around an element's content inside of any defined borders.

  • Margin is used to define the space around the elements, it clears an area around the element.

  • Borders as the name imply is used to fix borders on an element. Under its properties we have: border-width,border-radius, border-color, border-style and they are used to specify the style, color and size of the boder of an element.
